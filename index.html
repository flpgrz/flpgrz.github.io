Microbiome-based disease prediction with multimodal variational information bottlenecks
Filippo Grazioli, Raman Siarheyeu, Giampaolo Pileggi, Andrea Meiser
pre-print, 2021
bioarXiv  /  bibtex  /  code

Adopting an information theoretic interpretation of deep neural networks, MVIB allows to compute a joint stochastic encoding of heterogeneous input data modalities. We use the MVIB to predict whether patients are affected by a certain disease by looking at their gut microbiome from different perspectives.
